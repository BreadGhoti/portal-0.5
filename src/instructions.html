PORTAL 0.5



Click to play

Move: WASD
Jump: SPACE
Look: MOUSE
Place blue portal: LEFT CLICK
Place orange portal: RIGHT CLICK
Get to the red cube to progress to the next level.
Get to the blue cube to go back to the previous level.

Note: The game will run better when your browser is zoomed in. This effectively reduces the resolution, but can help if your frames are low. We personally default to 150% on a 1080p screen.
Additional Note: there may be frame stutter or lag due to cleanup at some times (so that this doesn't take 8GB of memory to run), so please be patient. Usually the level will stop lagging once you look around a bit (after the textures reload)
