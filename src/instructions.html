PORTAL 0.5



Click to play

Move: WASD, Jump: SPACE, Look: MOUSE
Place blue portal: LEFT CLICK
Place orange portal: RIGHT CLICK
Toggle zoom: MIDDLE CLICK
Get to the red cube to progress to the next level.
Get to the blue cube to go back to the previous level.
Falling off with no hope of return will restart the level.
Portals can only be placed on certain surfaces.

Note: The game will run better when your browser is zoomed in. This effectively reduces the resolution, but can help if your frames are low. We personally default to 150% on a 1080p screen.
Additional Note: if any crashes happen (mostly black screen and no movement), this is most likely an audio crash, which we unfortunately can't handle and you'll probably have to reload :(
